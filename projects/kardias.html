Kardias

A booking engine for Kardias Luxury Suites

https://kardias.gr
  • 3ll team
  • UI/UX
  • Branding
  • Illustrations
  • Graphic Designer
  • Web App

Kardias provides Luxury Suites in Nea Irakleitsa, Greece. The 3ll team was tasked to bring a fresh look to the existing static website and create a booking engine. As a result, visitors can quickly check the availability and pricing of the suites. They also have access to all the suite's information (amenities, photographs, etc.) and the surrounding area (sightseeing, restaurants, beaches, etc.).

My role in the project

In the 3ll team, I have the role of the Creative Director & Graphic Designer. That means that I oversee the overall spirit of the project, like aesthetics and brand awareness, and I also undertake anything design-related, from UI/UX and prototypes to logos and prints.

Tools that were utilized

Figma was used for wireframing, prototyping, and designing the UI/UX. Designed all the necessary vector-based material in Illustrator, such as logo, illustrations and iconography. Created mockups in Photoshop.
